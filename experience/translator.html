During Summer of 2016, I asked for a volunteer work at FPT Telecom, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. I got assigned to positions of a Movie Translator and also a Content Reviewer, in which I collaborated with the Content Team of 7 professional colleagues in the field of Communication and Media to control, validate and peer-review contents uploaded on a provided web server of FPT Play.

Due to the fact that the company had license on publishing Asian dramas but had no proper Vietnamese subtitles, I found English subtitles online and then translated those to Vietnamese. During the whole time, I translated Vietnamese subtitles for various Asian dramas using Subtitle Edit, namely See You Tomorrow, The Conmen in Vegas. I also wrote and updated the summary sections for each episode of a Chinese TV series called Princess Agents.
I also worked with my colleagues to peer-review all the content uploaded on the front page, including film review, comment sections. Moreover, I helped them validating the live schedules of the TV shows for the company's FPT Play Box.
