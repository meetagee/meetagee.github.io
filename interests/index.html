INTERESTS & ACTIVITIES

Apart from being a student who is grinding really hard on his academic life, I also have many other interests and activities that I usually spend time on whenever I am free!

Algorithms & Data structures

Algorithms and data structures are not only crucial for my learning and career but also a part of my interests.

As a Computer Engineering student, I am always fascinated by the variety of algorithms and their improvement in terms of space and time complexity.
When I am free, I sometimes read about applications and their related algorithms. Leetcode was a particular source for this interest of mine. The real life applications/interview questions provided there are not only helpful for my job-seeking process but also helps me a lot to improve my coding skills.

Embedded Systems & Applications

Embedded Systems are another field of interest of mine due to their portability, low-cost and most importantly, their variety in real-life applications.

I have been recently working a lot with embedded systems, MCUs such as ATmega328P, STM32 and low-level object-oriented C/C++. I found these systems extremely powerful, sustainable and portable. They consume low power and have lots of possibility for crucial real-life applications. I have actually seen various applications such as Home Security Systems, Smartwatches which are fascinating.
Also, the software comes along with embedded systems tend to be low-level and so, it really fits my purposes and brings me a challenge to become a better Computer Engineer in the future.

Mechanical Keyboards

I like Mechanical Keyboards very much. Their clicky sound and hearing just makes me feel great, especially when I have to type a lot for my code. Also, the idea of being able to customize your own keyboards via different switches, layouts, keycaps is particularly interesting to me!

I have tried most of the Cherry MX switches and am currently owning a Cooler Master Masterkeys Pro S and an Anne Pro 2. I would love to build a 60% Mechanical Keyboards soon in the future!
